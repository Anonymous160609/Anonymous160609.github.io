Brief of the Code:

It is an agent-based model including animated displays, real-time statistics in model , and parameter passings in interface. For a defined duration it simulates the movements of sheep and wolf, as well as their interactions between with grassland. Sheep and wolf will move around grassland. Sheep can consume and store grass, then average their grass sotre with other sheep nearby. Wolf can prey sheep, turn their colours into red, and stop movements of sheep.

Initializing the model.py in spyder, it will show its UI. Users can input parameters in the left panel then confirm and/or run model through buttons. When it runs, it is expected to show interactions between sheep, wolf, and grassland, both in form of animation and statistics.

Defination of ABM:

Agent-based Model simulates individual bodies' (agents') movements and interactions within in a given environment (wiki). It emphasizes randomness in each interaction and helps understand detailed, specific situations which would not be examinated and discussed by triditioanl methodologies e.g. regression.
